The Department of Civil Engineering of School of Engineering organized educational visit to 
Flyover Bridge at the olaiya intersection osogbo, osun state on 14th July. 2021 for Civil 
Engineering students to study different aspects of Bridge Engineering. Visit was organized as per 
the polytechnic  guidelines and recommendations regarding syllabus of  Civil Engineering.    
Visit was organized with the prior permission and guidance of the engineers working at the 
construction site & by the initiative and hard efforts of head of Civil Engineering department  
Engr. A.O Familusi which makes this visit a grand success. Students left the school  
Campus for visit on 14th July 2021 at 11.30 am. Students carefully studied and observed the 
different Parts of Bridge such as piers, bearing plate, abutment, bridge girder etc.
